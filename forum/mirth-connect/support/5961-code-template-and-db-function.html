                      Hi;

                      Channel scope means that its available only to the channel where you've mapped the variable.

                      Global means that its available to every channel on your Mirth Service, regardless as to where its mapped

                      I'm still not clear about globalChannel.

                      I discovered on the weekend that declaring channelMaps in the deploy processor elicited some odd behaviour (one of my variables was available at runtime, the other wasn't). I changed them to globalMaps and they worked fine.
